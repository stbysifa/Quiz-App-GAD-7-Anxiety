GAD-7 Anxiety Questionnaire

About This Application

This questionnaire is based on the GAD-7 scale, a validated tool used by healthcare professionals to screen for and measure the severity of generalized anxiety disorder. Your answers will provide insight into your anxiety symptoms over the past two weeks.

Note: This is not a diagnosis. If you have concerns, please seek advice from a qualified healthcare professional.
